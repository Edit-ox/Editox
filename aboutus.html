🎬 About Adhi Editz

Welcome to Adhi Editz, where creativity meets cinematic visuals. I am an aspiring video editor exploring movie edits, cinematic reels, anime clips, and YouTube short-form content. My focus is on improving my skills and creating clean, visually engaging edits that capture attention in the first few seconds.

This website was built by me using GitHub and ChatGPT as part of my learning journey in web development and digital creativity. With continuous learning and practice, I aim to produce edits that feel smooth, modern, and impactful.

🚀 My Journey

My editing journey started with a deep interest in movies and digital content. What began as simple experiments with short clips has gradually developed into a dedicated effort to build and improve my creative skills.

Creating this platform is also a step toward learning website development and building my own digital presence. Each project I work on helps me grow, learn new techniques, and refine my editing style.
